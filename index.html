Frequently Asked Questions

If you have any more questions feel free to ask them via E-Mail.

  • What is DeFi?

    DeFi stands for decentralized finance. It is a new way to generate passive income by investing in decentralized liquidity pools on the blockchain.

  • What are the risks?

    The main risk when investing in liquidity pools is that one side of the pool drops in value, however DeFi Investments only invests in USD backed liquidity pools. Meaning the only way loss will occur is by de-pegging of a stablecoin and or token or a drop in value of the US dollar.

  • When can I withdraw?

    You can request for your invested funds to be withdrawn at any time via E-Mail, however if withdrawn soon after depositing; the blockchain fees paid during the investing of the funds will likely result in a slight loss. It is recommended to keep funds invested for about two weeks to a month to start seeing noticeable profit. Withdrawing funds can also take up to two weeks due to possible blockchain network congestion.

  • What is the average APY?

    With the low risk (stable) liquidity pools DeFi Investments chooses to invest in, the average APY is ~27% anually. This can shift depending on the activity in the cryptocurrency market, regularly once assets start appreciating in value; investors tend to take funds out of stable liquidity pools to invest in speculative cryptocurrencies, meaning more rewards for those who stay in the pool, thus resulting in higher APY's for those remaining in the stable liquidity pools.

  • What is a liquidity pool?

    A liquidity pool is a decentralized collection (pool) of funds between two coins and/or tokens. Once a trader wants to initiate a swap between two assets, they need liquidity, someone who provides the other asset that they want to swap to. For this liquidity pools exist; investors invest funds into the liquidity pool which allows traders to always have liquidity to swap between coins and/or tokens. A transaction fee is required for this swap, which is paid to the liquidity providers. This way a nice APY is made for the liquidity providers.

  • How can I track the value of my investment?

    Investments in low risk stable liquidity pools like the ones DeFi Investments invests in are generally long term investments, thus we send our investors an update on their investment value every month.

    Currently we're working on an in-website interface for users to directly track their value.

  • What is the minimum and maximum investment amount?

    The minimum investment amount is $1,000 USD and the maximum investment amount is $100,000 USD. This maximum amount is defined by Dutch regulation for investment funds found in Article 5:3 in the Alternative Investment Fund Managers Directive (AIFM) guideline.
    The minimum investment amount is defined by the costs of managing the investment.
